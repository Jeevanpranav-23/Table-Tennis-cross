How to Play:

Controls for Laptop:

  • Player 1 (Top Paddle): Use S to move left and X to move right.
  • Player 2 (Bottom Paddle): Use Arrow Left to move left and Arrow Right to move right.

Controls for Phone:

  • Player 1 (Top Paddle): Touch and drag the top paddle to move it.
  • Player 2 (Bottom Paddle): Touch and drag the bottom paddle to move it.
